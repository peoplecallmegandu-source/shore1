Our Story

It began with a sunrise and a small roaster's curiosity. Aurora Coffee grew from a promise to honor farmers and to roast with intention. We believe coffee carries the imprint of place, and our job is to protect that fingerprint — from cherry to cup.

We partner with growers who share our values. Our relationships prioritize transparency, living wages, and regenerative practices. When you choose Aurora, you choose a direct path from farm to morning.

Sourcing

Direct trade partnerships and rigorous quality checks. Farmers receive annual premiums above market prices.

Roasting

Small batches. Precise profiles. Every roast is logged and studied to preserve origin clarity.

Community

We host monthly cuppings, barista training, and support local coffee initiatives.

Our Promise

Transparency, traceability, and taste. Every bag includes a farmer note, roast date, and tasting profile. We're committed to continuing to learn and to invest in those who grow our coffee.
